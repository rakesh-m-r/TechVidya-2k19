HUNTER's QUEST

Number of participants in each team is 4.
● Requirements & information about the game will be provided at the information desk.
● Each team will have to choose a leader and the leader will be answerable for anything in the team.
● Each team will be given a unique identification number.
● No member of the team should carry currency notes/electronic gadgets unless instructed to do so.
● The game consists of 3 rounds.
➢ The 1st round is called JUST GET IT.
➢ All the teams will be assigned a task of collecting some items.
➢ So, the teams after collecting the items have to assemble at the informed spot.
➢ The first 15 teams which comes and reports at the informed spot will be qualified for the next round.

*The 2nd Round is called FIGURE IT OUT. In 2nd round each team will be given with 10 different clues; So, each team has to find the clues to assemble at the particular informed place within the allotted time.
*The top 7teams which comes and reports will be qualified for the Final Round.

§ In the Final round also, each team will be given some clues.
§ Using these clues the teams have to find the treasure which is hidden.
§ The teams taking the help other than the team mates will be disqualified.
§ The teams should try to find the treasure through a fair means.
§ The team which finds the treasure first will be declared as the winner followed by the Runner.
