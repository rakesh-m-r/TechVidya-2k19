CTF(Capture the Flag)


Rules

A dedicated web site is designed for the ctf competition.

You would be given various basic questions on security and cryptography.

You would also be allowed to use the Internet.

You need to crack the given challenge and obtain the flag.

Submiting the flag leads you to the next level.

The one who captures all the flags the fastest and completes the challenge is the winner.

Certificates would be given to all the participants.

Top 3 individuals would be getting the winner certificates and cash prize!

Happy hacking! Enjoy the CTF!
