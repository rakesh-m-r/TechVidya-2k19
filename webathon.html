Webathon


Rules for Web Development:

1) Maximum of 3 participants in a team.

2) Any technologies can be used.

3) Drag and drop of applications is not allowed.

4) Word press and readymade templates are not allowed.

5) Participants need to design a 3+ pages website based on the competition topic.

6) Participants must present their website in front of the judges.

7) Coordinator’s instructions are to be followed.

Judgment Criteria:
Based on number of features, UX, UI, responsiveness.

The judge’s decision will be final. Any sort of argument with the judges shall lead to disqualification of the team.

Registration Fee: Rs.100/- per team
Venue: Lab 128 (CS & IS Block)
Time: 9:30 AM -10:30 AM
